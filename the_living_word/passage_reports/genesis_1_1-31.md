### Word Studies

#### בְּרֵאשִׁית (bereshit)

**See full word study:** [bereshit.md](../research/word_studies/bereshit.md)

**Summary:**
1. "In the beginning" (absolute, temporal) - traditional reading
2. "In the beginning of" (construct, relative) - "when God began to create"
3. "By way of beginning" - instrumental

**Historical Usage:**
- Occurs 50x in Hebrew Bible
- Usually "beginning of" (construct) in prophets/historical books
- Proverbs 8:22 - "YHWH created me as רֵאשִׁית of his way"

**Ancient Translations:**
- LXX: ἐν ἀρχῇ (en archē) - "in [the] beginning" (absolute)
- Vulgate: in principio
- Peshitta: b'reshit

**Modern Translations:**
- NRSV: "In the beginning"
- NJPS: "When God began to create"
- NIV: "In the beginning"

**Confidence:** **Probable** - "In the beginning" (MT vocalization), but construct reading plausible

**What is Lost:** The construct vs absolute ambiguity; potential allusion to Proverbs 8

#### אֱלֹהִים (Elohim)

**See full word study:** [elohim.md](../research/word_studies/elohim.md)

**Summary:**
- Plural form with singular meaning (when referring to Israel's God)
- Semantic range: supreme deity, divine council, gods, angels
- **Confidence:** Probable - Supreme deity in council context
- **Translation:** "Elohim" (preserve plural, don't flatten to "God")

## Phase F: Literary Analysis

### Structure

**Seven Days Framework:**
```
Day 1: Light / Day 4: Luminaries
Day 2: Sky/Sea / Day 5: Birds/Fish
Day 3: Land/Vegetation / Day 6: Animals/Humans
Day 7: Sabbath
```

**Chiastic Pattern:** Days 1-3 (kingdoms) parallel Days 4-6 (rulers)

**Key Literary Features:**
1. Repetition: "And God said... and it was so... and God saw that it was good"
2. Numerical patterns: 7 days, 10 utterances
3. Wordplay: אָדָם (adam/human) and אֲדָמָה (adamah/ground)

### Intertextuality

**Allusions/Quotations:**
- Psalm 33:6,9 - Creation by word
- Isaiah 45:18 - God created not תֹהוּ (tohu)
- Jeremiah 4:23 - תֹהוּ וָבֹהוּ (destruction imagery)

**Later Use:**
- Exodus 20:8-11 - Sabbath commandment
- Deuteronomy 5:12-15 - Alternative Sabbath rationale
