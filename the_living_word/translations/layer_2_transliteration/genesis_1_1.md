# Genesis 1:1

## Layer 2: Transliteration

```
bǝrēʾšît bārāʾ ʾĕlōhîm ʾēt haššāmayim wǝʾēt hāʾāreṣ
```

---

## Detailed Transliteration

| Hebrew | Transliteration | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| בְּרֵאשִׁית | bǝrēʾšît | sheva under bet; long ē; final silent t |
| בָּרָא | bārāʾ | qamets on both syllables; glottal stop at end |
| אֱלֹהִים | ʾĕlōhîm | reduced vowel ĕ; holem ô; hireq î |
| אֵת | ʾēt | tsere ē; used as direct object marker |
| הַשָּׁמַיִם | haššāmayim | definite article ha-; dagesh forte in shin |
| וְאֵת | wǝʾēt | waw conjunction with sheva; tsere on aleph |
| הָאָרֶץ | hāʾāreṣ | definite article hā-; qamets on aleph; final tsade |

---

## Phonetic Guide

- **bǝ** - like "be" in "bet" (reduced vowel)
- **rē** - long "eh" sound
- **ʾ** - glottal stop (catch in throat)
- **š** - "sh" as in "ship"
- **î** - long "ee" sound
- **ê** - long "ay" sound (like "say")
- **ā** - long "ah" sound
- **hā** - "ha" with emphasis
