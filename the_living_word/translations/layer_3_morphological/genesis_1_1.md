# Genesis 1:1

```
[בְּרֵאשִׁית] [בָּרָא] [אֱלֹהִים] [אֵת] [הַשָּׁמַיִם] [וְאֵת] [הָאָרֶץ]
```

---

## Word-by-Word Morphology

### 1. בְּרֵאשִׁית (bǝrēʾšît)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **בְּ** (bǝ) - Preposition: "in/at" (with sheva, reduced vowel)
- **רֵאשִׁית** (rēʾšît) - Noun: "beginning/head/start"
  - Root: ר-א-ש (R-ʾ-Š)
  - Form: feminine singular construct
  - State: construct (bound to implied noun)

**Parsing:** prep + noun-fs-construct
**Gloss:** "in beginning of"

---

### 2. בָּרָא (bārāʾ)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **Root:** ב-ר-א (B-R-ʾ)
- **Binyan:** Qal (simple active)
- **Tense:** Perfect (completed action)
- **Person:** 3rd
- **Gender:** Masculine
- **Number:** singular

**Parsing:** verb-Qal-perfect-3ms
**Gloss:** "he created"

**Note:** Divine-only verb - never used with human subject in Hebrew Bible

---

### 3. אֱלֹהִים (ʾĕlōhîm)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **Root:** א-ל-ה (ʾ-L-H)
- **Form:** Noun, masculine plural
- **Vowel pattern:** reduced vowel + holem + hireq-yod-mem
- **Syntactic function:** Subject of verb בָּרָא

**Parsing:** noun-mp
**Gloss:** "God/Elohim/divine powers"

**Note:** Plural form with singular verb (when referring to Israel's God)

---

### 4. אֵת (ʾēt)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **Form:** Particle
- **Function:** Direct object marker
- **Origin:** Possibly from root א-ת-ה (ʾ-T-H, "to come")

**Parsing:** DO-marker
**Gloss:** "[marks direct object]"

**Note:** Untranslatable in English; indicates accusative case

---

### 5. הַשָּׁמַיִם (haššāmayim)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **הַ** (ha) - Definite article: "the"
- **שָּׁמַיִם** (šāmayim) - Noun: "heavens/sky"
  - Root: ש-מ-ה (Š-M-H, "to be high")
  - Form: Masculine dual (pluralis excellentiae)
  - Dagesh forte in ש from article assimilation

**Parsing:** art + noun-md
**Gloss:** "the heavens"

---

### 6. וְאֵת (wǝʾēt)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **וְ** (wǝ) - Conjunction: "and" (with sheva)
- **אֵת** (ʾēt) - Direct object marker

**Parsing:** conj + DO-marker
**Gloss:** "and [marks direct object]"

---

### 7. הָאָרֶץ (hāʾāreṣ)
**Morphological breakdown:**
- **הָ** (hā) - Definite article: "the" (with qamets before aleph)
- **אָרֶץ** (ʾāreṣ) - Noun: "earth/land"
  - Root: uncertain
  - Form: Feminine singular

**Parsing:** art + noun-fs
**Gloss:** "the earth/land"

---

## Syntactic Structure

```
[בְּרֵאשִׁית]              - Temporal phrase (when/in the beginning)
    [בָּרָא]               - Verb (main predicate)
        [אֱלֹהִים]         - Subject (agent of creation)
    [אֵת הַשָּׁמַיִם]      - Direct object #1 (what was created)
    [וְאֵת הָאָרֶץ]       - Direct object #2 (coordinated with waw)
```

**Clause Type:** Verbal clause (predicate-initial in Hebrew)

**Translation Challenges:**
1. בְּרֵאשִׁית absolute vs. construct state
2. אֱלֹהִים singular vs. plural meaning
3. אֵת untranslatable DO marker
