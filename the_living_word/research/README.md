# Years of Life Reconstruction

## Yeshua's Ministry Timeline

### Year 30 - Baptism and Early Ministry
- Location: Judea, Galilee
- Events: Baptism by Yochanan, temptation, first disciples
- Sources: Mark 1, Matthew 3-4, Luke 3-4, John 1
- Confidence: **Directly attested**

### Years 31-32 - Galilean Ministry
- Location: Galilee, Capernaum
- Events: Teaching, healings, parables, discipleship
- Sources: Synoptic gospels
- Confidence: **Highly probable**

### Year 33 - Final Journey and Passion
- Location: Judea, Jerusalem
- Events: Triumphal entry, Last Supper, crucifixion, resurrection
- Sources: All four gospels, Pauline epistles
- Confidence: **Directly attested**

## The Missing Years (Age 30-33 gaps)

### Questions
1. What happened between baptism and Galilean ministry?
2. How many Passovers did Yeshua attend?
3. What was the duration of the ministry?

### Traditional Reconstruction
- **One year ministry** (Synoptic chronology compressed)
- **Three year ministry** (John's multiple Passovers)
