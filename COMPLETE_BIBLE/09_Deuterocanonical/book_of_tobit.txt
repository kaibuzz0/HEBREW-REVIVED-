================================================================================
BOOK OF TOBIT
Category: 09 Deuterocanonical
================================================================================

1 This book tells the story of Tobit, son of Tobiel... I Tobit have walked all the days of my life in the ways of truth and righteousness.

2 Now when I was come home again... I said to my son, Go and bring whatsoever poor man you find.

3 Then I being grieved did weep, and in my sorrow prayed... And Tobias took his journey, and the dog went with him.

5 So they went forth both, and the young man's dog with them.

6 And as they went on their journey, they came in the evening to the river Tigris... And the angel said to him, Take the fish, and the heart and liver and gall.

7 Raguel's daughter Sara... And Raguel gave him his daughter Sara.

8 And he took the heart and liver of the fish, and put them upon the coals... And the angel said, Take hold of her, and fear not.

11 And Tobias went in, and his eyes were opened... And he saw his son Tobias.

12 And Raguel and Edna his wife came forth and blessed Tobias... I am Raphael, one of the seven holy angels.

14 And Tobit wrote a prayer of rejoicing... Wherefore I say unto you, keep the ways of the Lord.
