THIRD BOOK OF MACCABEES
Category: 09 Deuterocanonical

1 When Philopater... Entered the holy of holies.

2 The Jews were gathered... To the hippodrome.

3 Eleazar... The aged priest.

4 The king sent... To bring the elephants.

5 But the Lord... Sent an angel.

6 And the king... Changed his mind.
