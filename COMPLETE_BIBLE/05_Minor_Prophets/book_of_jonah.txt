================================================================================
BOOK OF JONAH
Category: 05 Minor Prophets
================================================================================

1 Now the word of the LORD came to Jonah the son of Amittai, saying, Arise, go to Nineveh, that great city, and call out against it, for their evil has come up before me. But Jonah rose to flee to Tarshish from the presence of the LORD.

2 I called out to the LORD, out of my distress, and he answered me; out of the belly of Sheol I cried, and you heard my voice... Salvation belongs to the LORD!

3 Then the word of the LORD came to Jonah the second time, saying, Arise, go to Nineveh, that great city, and call out against it the message that I tell you... And the people of Nineveh believed God. They called for a fast and put on sackcloth.

4 But it displeased Jonah exceedingly, and he was angry... And should not I pity Nineveh, that great city, in which there are more than 120,000 persons who do not know their right hand from their left, and also much cattle?
