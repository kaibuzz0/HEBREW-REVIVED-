LETTER OF PETER TO PHILIP
Category: 07 Epistles

1 Peter, the apostle of Jesus Christ... To Philip, our beloved brother.

5 And Peter said... The Lord appeared to us.

10 And the Lord said... Go and preach the gospel.

15 And Philip said... I will go, but help me.
