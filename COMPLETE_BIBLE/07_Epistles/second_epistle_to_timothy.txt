================================================================================
SECOND EPISTLE TO TIMOTHY
Category: 07 Epistles
================================================================================

1 I thank God whom I serve, as did my ancestors, with a clear conscience, as I remember you constantly in my prayers night and day... For God gave us a spirit not of fear but of power and love and self-control.

3 All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness, that the man of God may be complete, equipped for every good work.

4 I have fought the good fight, I have finished the race, I have kept the faith. Henceforth there is laid up for me the crown of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous judge, will award to me on that day, and not only to me but also to all who have loved his appearing.


[End of second_epistle_to_timothy]
