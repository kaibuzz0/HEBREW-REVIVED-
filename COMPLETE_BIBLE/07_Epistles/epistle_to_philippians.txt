================================================================================
EPISTLE TO PHILIPPIANS
Category: 07 Epistles
================================================================================

1 I thank my God in all my remembrance of you... And I am sure of this, that he who began a good work in you will bring it to completion at the day of Jesus Christ.

2 Do nothing from selfish ambition or conceit, but in humility count others more significant than yourselves. Let each of you look not only to his own interests, but also to the interests of others. Have this mind among yourselves, which is yours in Christ Jesus...

3 Indeed, I count everything as loss because of the surpassing worth of knowing Christ Jesus my Lord. For his sake I have suffered the loss of all things and count them as rubbish, in order that I may gain Christ.

4 Rejoice in the Lord always; again I will say, rejoice. Let your reasonableness be known to everyone. The Lord is at hand; do not be anxious about anything, but in everything by prayer and supplication with thanksgiving let your requests be made known to God.


[End of epistle_to_philippians]
