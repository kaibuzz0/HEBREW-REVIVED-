================================================================================
EPISTLE TO PHILEMON
Category: 07 Epistles
================================================================================

1 I thank my God always when I remember you in my prayers, because I hear of your love and of the faith that you have toward the Lord Jesus and for all the saints... Perhaps this is why he was parted from you for a while, that you might have him back forever, no longer as a bondservant but more than a bondservant, as a beloved brother.


[End of epistle_to_philemon]
