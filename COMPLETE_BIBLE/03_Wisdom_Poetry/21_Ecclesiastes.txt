================================================================================
THE BOOK OF 21 ECCLESIASTES
================================================================================

The words of the Preacher, the son of David, king in Jerusalem. Vanity of vanities, says the Preacher, vanity of vanities! All is vanity.

For everything there is a season, and a time for every matter under heaven: a time to be born, and a time to die; a time to plant, and a time to pluck up what is planted.

Two are better than one, because they have a good reward for their toil. For if they fall, one will lift up his fellow. But woe to him who is alone when he falls and has not another to lift him up! And though a man might prevail against one who is alone, two will withstand him - a threefold cord is not quickly broken.

Remember also your Creator in the days of your youth... The end of the matter; all has been heard. Fear God and keep his commandments, for this is the whole duty of man.
