The Gospel of Mary

...the Savior made her worthy...

Jesus said, 'Blessed is one who exists before coming into being.'

'Listen to this: where the mind is, there is the treasure.'

Mary said, 'You have spoken these things to us in parables.'

Jesus said, 'Blessed is one who seeks.'

Mary turned to the inward, she saw a vision.

She saw the soul ascending past the seven powers.

The first power said, 'I am Darkness.'

The second said, 'I am Desire.'

The third said, 'I am Ignorance.'

The fourth said, 'I am Envy of Death.'

The fifth said, 'I am the Domain of the Flesh.'

The sixth said, 'I am the Foolish Wisdom of the Flesh.'

The seventh said, 'I am Wrathful Wisdom.'

Then the soul said, 'What binds me is slain.'

And she rose up to the Eighth Sphere.

And she saw the Rest.

Mary turned outward, she came forth.

She spoke to the disciples: 'I saw the Lord.'

'And he spoke these things to me.'

The disciples were grieved...

Andrew spoke: 'Say what you think...'

'...but we know the Savior did not speak this way.'

Peter arose, he said: 'Did he really speak with a woman...'

'...privately from us and not openly?'

'Shall we turn about and all listen to her?'

'Did he prefer her to us?'

Mary wept, she said: 'My brothers...'

'I saw him, I listened to him.'

Levi spoke: 'Sister, we know...'

'The Savior made her worthy.'

'Let us be ashamed and put on...'

'...the Perfect Human and go forth...'

'...as he commanded us.'

'And we will become as he is.'

The disciples went forth to preach.

And Mary remained, she spoke...

The Savior loved her more than...

'...and we are to follow her example.'

'The perfection is...'

'...and the rest is silence.'
