================================================================================
THE BOOK OF 08 RUTH
================================================================================

In the days when the judges ruled there was a famine in the land... But Ruth said, Do not urge me to leave you or to return from following you. For where you go I will go, and where you lodge I will lodge. Your people shall be my people, and your God my God.

So she set out and went and gleaned in the field after the reapers, and she happened to come to the part of the field belonging to Boaz.

So Boaz took Ruth, and she became his wife. And he went in to her, and the LORD gave her conception, and she bore a son... They named him Obed. He was the father of Jesse, the father of David.
