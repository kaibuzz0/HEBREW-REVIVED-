================================================================================
TESTAMENTS OF TWELVE PATRIARCHS
Category: 13 Other
================================================================================

1 The copy of the testament of Reuben... I was the first-born... and by my father's command I was the first to see the destruction of Joseph.

2 The copy of the words of Simeon... I was born of Bilhah... and I confess my jealousy against Joseph my brother.

3 The copy of the words of Levi... I was born in Haran... and I was zealous for the Lord's sake.

4 The copy of the words of Judah... I was the fourth son... and the Lord gave me favor.

5 The copy of the words of Issachar... I was the fifth son... and I was a husbandman.

6 The copy of the words of Zebulun... I was the sixth son... and I was a sailor.

7 The copy of the words of Dan... I was the seventh son... and I was a serpent.

8 The copy of the words of Naphtali... I was the eighth son... and I was a swift runner.

9 The copy of the words of Gad... I was the ninth son... and I loved not my brother.

10 The copy of the words of Asher... I was the tenth son... and I was blessed.

11 The copy of the words of Joseph... I was the eleventh son... and the Lord was with me.

12 The copy of the words of Benjamin... I was the twelfth son... and I was a ravenous wolf.


[End of testaments_of_twelve_patriarchs]
