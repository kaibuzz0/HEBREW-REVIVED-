================================================================================
PRAYER OF MANASSEH
Category: 13 Other
================================================================================

1 O Lord, Almighty God of our fathers... I have sinned above the number of the sands of the sea.

1 For I have set up abominations... And now I bend the knee of my heart, beseeching you.

1 O Lord, according to your great goodness... Now therefore I bow the knee.


[End of prayer_of_manasseh]
